Shrek: A Tribute

A lovely picture of Shrek in his natural state.

Shrek, bless his sweet name, is a loveable green ogre who lives in a swamp outside the land of Far, Far, Away. Often found amongst his best friends, Donkey and Puss in Boots, Shrek enjoys his free time cooking a number of delectable foods, including onion stew, eyeball creme brulee, and moldy pumpkin pudding. Recently wed to Fiona the Ogre, Shrek and Fiona are expecting three little ones to join their swamp home.

Shrek's Achievements:

  • Hollywood Walk of Fame Star Recipient
  • Named one of the "100 Greatest Characters of the Last 20 Years"
